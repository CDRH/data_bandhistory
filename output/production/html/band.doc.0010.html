COLORADO SHOW

  • 1. Hash to hash on Hail Nebraska
  • 2. Form BUFFALO on Glory, Glory, Colorado
  • 3. Play Bb9 chord and two Bunny Hops into diamonds
  • 4. Around diamond on double eagle
    4. Circles on Chief of Staff 16 measures
  • 5. Into Hexagon on March of Cornhuskers 8 measures without into
  • 6. Around Hexagon and to the rear on Champions
  • 7. Form Company Fronts on March of Cornhuskers
    play 4 measures 4 dead counts play next 4 measures
  • 8. Play and Sing Band Song and Hail Varsity
  • 9. On last half of Heil Varsity return to Hexagon
  • 10. ON Thunder and Blazes trio Perpendiculars go out 16 counts to the rear and return when back do circles around every 4th man for last half of Trio
  • 11. Back to Diamonds on March of Cornhusker 8 measures and stop play chord
  • 12. Into Lyre on Double eagle
  • 13. Play three Kings
  • 14. Off on No Place

Together turn 1 - 2
