HALFTIME ROUTINE

ATTENTION AT SIDELINE

  • I Present 2-3-4; Inst. up 2-3-4; Play ON THE SQUARE 1st and 2nd strains (No repeat)
  • II TRIO ON THE SQUARE for circles
    Present 2-3-4 at end of TRIO
  • III Drums for 8 measures. At end of drums Band does KICK (except 1-2-10-11 who kneel) 2-3-4
  • IV Inst. up 2-3-4 + announcement GRANDIOSO INTRO. TO TRIO.
    Present 2-3-4; -1-2-10-11 up 2-3-4.
  • V To the roar on drums 24 measures. After drums leg KICK 2-3-4 for everyone.
    Inst. up 2-3-4 + announcement
  • VI MISTER TOUCHDOWN U.S.A.
    NO PAUSE
  • VII TRIO THUNDER AND BLAZES into Steerhead 32 measures
    After halt - present 2-3-4; Face (East) 2-3-4 Kneel 2-3-4. Inst. up 2-3-4
    WAIT for downbeat from Lentz here. THIS IS THE FIRST TIME THERE WILL BE A PAUSE.
  • VIII EYES OF TEXAS
    After Eyes of Texas - present 2-3-4 and wait. On next command from Lentz up 2-3-4. Face 2-3-4. Inst. up 2-3-4 and into Texas
  • IX Trio THUNDER AND BLAZES 30 measure to form Texas
    In Texas - Present 2-3-4; Kneel 2-3-4; Present 2-3-4 * announcement
    PLAY YELLOW ROSE.
    Starts with Drums.
    After Yellow Rose - Present 2-3-4 + announcement. Up 2-3-4; Face 2-3-4; Inst. up 2-3-4
  • X Trio MOVE ON THUNDER AND BLAZES INTO N U
    When in place - Present 2-3-4 (Hold horns with left hands)
    Face (North) 2-3-4
    RAISE hates with right hands up in 4 counts slowly, down in 4 counts;
    slowly watch Terry. (Basses stand still)
  • XI On Command from Terry
    Inst. up 2-3-4; MOVE N U 10 yds. north on NO PLACE
    ALL FLANK LEFT FOOT OVER RIGHT FOOT AND OFF!!!!!!! !
